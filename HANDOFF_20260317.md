## 4. What was tried and what failed

### A. Full-body perception-driven unified reconstruction

This was attempted multiple times. It captured more hub shape, but often produced:

- multi-solid / compound output
- broken or fragmented geometry
- STEP imported by SolidWorks as assembly-like structure

Do not return to that path unless the explicit goal is to debug full-body revolve again.

Relevant historical markers:

- `Unified full-body modeling enabled. Reverting to perception-driven full rotary reconstruction.`
- older outputs around `wheel_20260316_212100.step` and earlier

### B. Hub replacement by shell substitution

This produced visible perception traces but also created layered/stacked hub appearance and unstable transitions.

It was later abandoned in favor of the guarded-section revolve base + post features path.
